# 🌍 Zero Waste Vission

A smart, community-driven platform designed to minimize waste, promote sustainable habits, and connect individuals with local recycling and waste management resources. Our mission is to make eco-friendly living accessible, trackable, and impactful for everyone.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Smart Waste Classifier:** Easily categorize your trash into biodegradable, recyclable, or hazardous waste using AI-powered insights.
* **Community Eco-Hub:** Connect with local environmental drives, share zero-waste tips, and participate in sustainability challenges.
* **Habit & Goal Tracker:** Set personal waste-reduction targets and track your daily eco-friendly habits in a digital journal interface.
* **Resource Locator:** Find nearby recycling centers, composting hubs, and zero-waste stores seamlessly.

---
